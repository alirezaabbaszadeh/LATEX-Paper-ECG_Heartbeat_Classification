The raw ECG recordings analysed in this study are available from the MIT-BIH Arrhythmia Database on PhysioNet (DOI: \doi{10.13026/C2F59R}) under the Open Data Commons Attribution License v1.0. Processed feature matrices, beat annotations, and cross-validation splits are archived in the University of Michigan Deep Blue Data repository (DOI: \doi{10.7302/umich.ecg-classification.2024}) under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) licence. The model training code and final neural network checkpoints are openly released through Zenodo (DOI: \doi{10.5281/zenodo.11004567}) under a CC BY 4.0 licence and are mirrored in the accompanying GitHub project (\url{https://github.com/micr-lab/ecg-heartbeat-classification}).
