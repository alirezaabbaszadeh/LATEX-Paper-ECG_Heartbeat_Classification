\title{ECG Heartbeat Classification under AAMI Mapping using a CNN+Conformer with Wavelet Scalograms: A Reproducible Pipeline}

\begin{abstract}
\textbf{Objective:} Provide a compact, fully reproducible ECG classification pipeline under the AAMI five-class taxonomy with time--frequency front-end and attention-based classifier.\newline
\textbf{Methods:} Beat windows are transformed to Morlet CWT scalograms and fed to a CNN+Conformer. Class-weighting mitigates imbalance; evaluation includes macro/weighted F1 alongside PR/ROC.\newline
\textbf{Results:} All tables/figures are derived from existing repository artifacts pinned to a public commit; additional figures (leaderboard, per-class comparisons) are generated from classification reports.\newline
\textbf{Significance:} The artifact-driven evaluation offers transparent, reproducible baselines that can be integrated into clinical research workflows and embedded devices without retraining.
\end{abstract}
