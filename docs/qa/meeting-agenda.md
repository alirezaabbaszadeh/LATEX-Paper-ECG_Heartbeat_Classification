## Agenda

1. **Opening** (5 min)
   - Welcome & recap of previous meeting outcomes
   - Confirm agenda and timeboxes
2. **Action Item Review** (10 min)
   - Walk through open action items
   - Assign owners and due dates for any carry-overs
3. **Paper Status Roundtable** (20 min)
   - `Paper Journal A`
   - `Paper Journal B`
   - Metrics dashboard / blocker discussion
4. **Rotating Deep Dive** (15 min)
   - Topic:
   - Materials:
   - Decisions / follow-ups:
5. **Risk & Issue Log** (10 min)
   - New issues raised since last sync
   - Mitigation plans & owners
6. **Planning & Next Steps** (10 min)
   - Upcoming deadlines & submission milestones
   - Confirm next meeting date and prep expectations
7. **Wrap-up** (5 min)
   - Decisions recap
   - Action item summary
   - Parking lot / items to revisit
